🎯 How to Play

• Get close to the ball to control it

• Press SPACE or KICK to shoot the ball

• Ball bounces off you in the direction you're facing

• Score on the RIGHT goal (yellow)

• Defend your LEFT goal from the enemy team

• First team to reach the score limit wins!

• Each goal adds +5 seconds to your timer
